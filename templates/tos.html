When you use Annoy-Friend, we collect the following information:

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the email automation service

We implement security measures to protect your information:

Annoy-Friend uses Google API Services and is subject to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
