What is this?

Annoy-Friend is a Node.js-powered web app that lets you send automated, playful emails to your friends. Simply sign up, log in, and create up to 10 different emails that will be sent automatically to your chosen recipient.

How does it work?

  1. Sign up for an account.
  2. Log in to your dashboard.
  3. Add your email so the app can send emails on your behalf.
  4. Create up to 10 automated emails for your friend.
  5. Let the app do the rest!
