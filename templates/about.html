About Annoy-Friend

What is Annoy-Friend?

Annoy-Friend is the ultimate platform for sending automated, playful emails to your friends. Because sometimes, a little friendly annoyance is exactly what your friendships need!

Features

Automated Email Campaigns

Create up to 10 different email templates that automatically send to your friends at scheduled intervals. Set it and forget it!

Secure Gmail Integration

Built with Google's official Gmail API and OAuth2 authentication. Your emails are sent securely from your own Gmail account.

Easy Setup

Simple signup process with Google authentication. Get started in minutes, not hours. No complicated configuration required.

How It Works

Getting started with Annoy-Friend is simple:

  1. Sign Up with Google: Create your account using secure Google OAuth2 authentication. We'll never store your password.
  2. Create Email Templates: Design up to 10 different email templates with custom messages, subjects, and scheduling options.
  3. Add Your Friends: Enter your friends' email addresses and assign them to different email campaigns. Don't worry, it's all in good fun!
  4. Watch the Magic Happen: Sit back and relax as your automated emails bring smiles (and maybe eye-rolls) to your friends' inboxes.

Technology Stack

Annoy-Friend is built using modern, secure technologies:

  • Backend: Node.js & Express.js
  • Email Service: Gmail API with OAuth2
  • Database: MongoDB for user data & templates
  • Authentication: Google OAuth2
  • Frontend: Responsive HTML5 & CSS3
  • Security: Industry-standard encryption

Important Note

Annoy-Friend is designed for harmless fun between friends. Please use this platform responsibly and only send emails to people who know you and will appreciate the humor. We do not condone spam, harassment, or any malicious use of our service. Always respect others' privacy and email preferences.
